## Walking in Divine Purpose

Every believer has a unique calling and purpose in God's kingdom. In this powerful message, we explore how to discover and walk in the divine purpose that God has prepared for each of us.

### Key Points:

1. **Understanding Your Identity in Christ**
   - You are fearfully and wonderfully made
   - God has a specific plan for your life
   - Your identity shapes your destiny

2. **Discovering Your Calling**
   - Prayer and seeking God's will
   - Recognizing your gifts and talents
   - Listening to the Holy Spirit's guidance

3. **Walking in Purpose**
   - Taking steps of faith
   - Overcoming obstacles and challenges
   - Staying aligned with God's will

### Scripture References:

- Jeremiah 29:11 - "For I know the plans I have for you," declares the Lord
- Ephesians 2:10 - We are God's handiwork, created for good works
- Romans 8:28 - All things work together for good

### Prayer Points:

- Ask God to reveal His purpose for your life
- Pray for wisdom and discernment
- Seek courage to step into your calling
