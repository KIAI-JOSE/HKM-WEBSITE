**About Overseer Dr. Joseph**

Overseer Dr. Joseph is a senior leader and overseer at **HKM Ministries International**. With extensive experience in ministry leadership, he has devoted his life to advancing the Gospel, equipping believers, and strengthening churches through faithful teaching and pastoral care.

**Ministry Journey**

Overseer Dr. Joseph’s ministry began with a deep sense of calling to serve the Lord in full-time ministry. After years of pastoral involvement and leadership development, he assumed key roles in church planting, discipleship, and ministry coordination before being appointed as an overseer within HKM Ministries International. His journey reflects commitment, spiritual maturity, and a passion for seeing lives transformed by Christ.

**Education & Credentials**

Overseer Dr. Joseph holds advanced theological and ministerial qualifications, enabling him to lead with insight and integrity. His credentials include:

1. Doctor of Ministry (D.Min)
2. Master of Ministry and Theology
3. Bachelor of Theology (B.Th)
4. Ministerial Leadership Certification

**Vision & Mission**

Overseer Dr. Joseph’s vision is to see believers empowered to live out their faith boldly and to help build strong, healthy churches rooted in Scripture. He is passionate about:

\* Equipping leaders for effective ministry

\* Encouraging spiritual growth and maturity

\* Promoting unity among church communities

\* Advancing outreach and discipleship initiatives

** Family**

Overseer Dr. Joseph is a dedicated family man. He is married to Rev. mary  and blessed with two children. Outside of ministry, he enjoys studying the Bible, mentoring upcoming leaders, and engaging in community service.
