## About Rev. Dr. John Doe

Rev. Dr. John Doe is the founding Senior Pastor of HKM Ministries International. With over 20 years of ministry experience, he has dedicated his life to spreading the Gospel and building up the body of Christ.

### Ministry Journey

Pastor John's journey began in his early twenties when he received a clear call from God to enter full-time ministry. After completing his theological education, he served in various capacities before founding HKM Ministries International.

### Education & Credentials

- Doctor of Divinity, International Bible College
- Master of Theology, Nairobi Theological Seminary
- Bachelor of Arts in Biblical Studies

### Vision & Mission

Pastor John's vision is to see lives transformed by the power of God's Word. He is passionate about:
- Equipping believers for ministry
- Reaching the lost with the Gospel
- Building strong families
- Developing future leaders

### Family

Pastor John is married to his beloved wife, Grace, and they have three children. He enjoys spending time with his family, reading, and mentoring young ministers.
