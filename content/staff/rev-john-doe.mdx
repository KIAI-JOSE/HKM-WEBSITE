**About Bishop Dr. George**

Bishop George is the founding Presiding Bishop of **HKM Ministries International**. With many years of dedicated ministry service, he has committed his life to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing believers, and strengthening the body of Christ across nations.

**Ministry Journey**

Bishop George’s ministry journey began after a divine calling to serve God wholeheartedly. Through prayer, mentorship, and hands-on ministry experience, he grew in spiritual leadership and pastoral responsibility. Over the years, he served in various leadership roles within the church before establishing HKM Ministries International with a vision to impact lives globally.

**Education & Credentials**

Bishop George is a Doctor of Theology and Ministry and has undergone extensive theological and ministerial training, equipping him with strong biblical foundations and leadership excellence. His credentials include:

*  Doctor of Theology and Ministry
* Master of Theology
* Diploma in Theology and Ministry Studies
* Episcopal Ordination and Ministerial Certification

**Vision & Mission**

Bishop George’s vision is to see lives transformed through the power of God’s Word and the work of the Holy Spirit. He is passionate about:

* Evangelism and soul-winning
* Discipleship and spiritual maturity
* Leadership training and mentorship
* Church growth and community impact

**Family**

Bishop George is a devoted husband and father who values strong Christian family principles. He is married to Rev. Phyllis and blessed with three children. He enjoys prayer, studying Scripture, mentoring young ministers, and serving the wider Christian community.
