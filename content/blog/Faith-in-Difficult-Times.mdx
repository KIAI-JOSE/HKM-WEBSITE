# **Faith in Difficult Times**

#### **Opening Prayer**

Lord, we thank You for Your presence with us even in trials. Strengthen our faith today. Amen.

#### **Scripture Reading**

Isaiah 41:10

#### **Message**

Difficult moments test our faith, but God promises never to leave us. When we trust Him during hard times, He gives us peace and strength to overcome.

#### **Reflection Questions**

What challenge are you facing right now?

How can you trust God more in this situation?

#### **Closing Prayer**

Father, strengthen our faith when we feel weak. Help us rely on You always. Amen.
