# **Living a Thankful Life**

#### **Opening Prayer**

Lord, thank You for all Your blessings. Teach us to live with gratitude. Amen.

#### **Scripture Reading**

1 Thessalonians 5:18

#### **Message**

Thankfulness shifts our focus from problems to God’s goodness. When we give thanks in all situations, our hearts are filled with joy and peace.

#### **Reflection Questions**

What are you thankful for today?

How can you express gratitude daily?

#### **Closing Prayer**

Lord, help us to always be thankful, no matter the situation. Amen.
