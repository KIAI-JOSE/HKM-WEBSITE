# About HKM Ministries International

Welcome to HKM Ministries International, a vibrant community of believers located in the heart of Utawala, Mihango, Nairobi, Kenya. We are a church that believes in the transformative power of God's love and the importance of building authentic relationships with Jesus Christ and one another.

## Our History

HKM Ministries International was founded with a vision to create a place where people from all walks of life could encounter God's love, grow in their faith, and discover their purpose. What started as a small gathering has grown into a thriving community that impacts lives both locally and internationally.

## Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, love people, and serve the world.

## Our Vision

To be a church that transforms lives, strengthens families, and impacts communities through the power of the Gospel.

## Our Core Values

### **Faith**
We believe in the authority of Scripture and the power of prayer. Our faith is the foundation of everything we do.

### **Family**
We are committed to building strong, healthy families and creating a church family where everyone belongs.

### **Fellowship**
We value authentic relationships and community. We believe life is better when we do it together.

### **Service**
We are called to serve God by serving others, both within our church and in our community.

### **Excellence**
We strive for excellence in all we do, giving our best as an offering to God.

## What We Believe

- The Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God
- Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ
- The Trinity: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
- The importance of baptism and communion
- The gifts of the Holy Spirit are active today
- The Great Commission to make disciples

## Our Ministries

We offer various ministries designed to help people grow in their relationship with God:

- **Sunday Worship Services** - Dynamic worship and biblical teaching
- **Youth Ministry** - Empowering the next generation
- **Children's Ministry** - Building strong foundations
- **Men's Ministry** - Developing godly men
- **Women's Ministry** - Encouraging and equipping women
- **Outreach Programs** - Serving our community

## Join Our Family

Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for years, we invite you to join our church family. Come as you are, and discover how God wants to use you to make a difference in this world.

We look forward to meeting you and walking alongside you on your spiritual journey!
