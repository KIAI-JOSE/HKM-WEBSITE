## Annual Youth Conference 2023

Join us for an incredible 3-day youth conference that will ignite your passion for God and equip you for your divine calling!

### Event Details

**Dates:** November 15-17, 2023\
**Time:** 6:00 PM each evening\
**Location:** HKM Conference Center\
**Ages:** 13-25 years old

### What to Expect

* **Powerful Worship** - Experience God's presence through contemporary worship
* **Inspiring Messages** - Hear from dynamic speakers who will challenge and encourage you
* **Interactive Workshops** - Learn practical skills for Christian living
* **Fellowship & Fun** - Connect with other young believers and build lasting friendships

### Conference Theme: "Ignited for Purpose"

This year's theme focuses on discovering and walking in your God-given purpose. You'll learn how to:

* Identify your spiritual gifts and talents
* Overcome obstacles that hold you back
* Make a difference in your community
* Build a strong foundation for your future

### Special Guests

* **Pastor Michael Johnson** - Youth Pastor from Nairobi Central Church
* **Sarah Wanjiku** - Christian recording artist and worship leader
* **David Kimani** - Motivational speaker and author

### Registration

**Early Bird:** KSh 1,500 (before November 1st)\
**Regular:** KSh 2,000\
**Includes:** All sessions, materials, and refreshments

### What to Bring

* Bible and notebook
* Comfortable clothing
* Heart ready to encounter God

Don't miss this life-changing event! Register today and invite your friends to join you for an unforgettable weekend with God.
